About Us

The Feature Selection Evaluation Framework is a standardized benchmarking framework designed to provide rigorous, transparent, and reproducible evaluations of feature selection algorithms across diverse datasets.

Our tool centralizes performance metrics such as Accuracy, AUC, Clustering Accuracy, and NMI, allowing researchers to visualize how different methods scale as the percentage of selected features increases.

Contribute to the Benchmark

We believe in open science. If you have developed a new feature selection method, we invite you to include it in our global leaderboard.

To participate, please use our benchmark code to evaluate your method and send your .csv results along with your source code files to:

fseval@imada.sdu.dk

After a brief validation process by our team at SDU, your results will be added to the official dashboard for the community to see, alongside with your scientific paper on the references page.
